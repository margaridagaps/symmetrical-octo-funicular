How different can memories be about the same things? This research study will investigate the way memories are perceived and seen, through understanding how they’re manifested visually.

The information and material gathered from this website is meant to serve an academic research project only. Before you start navigating, please note that - if you choose to participate in this research project, any material you submit has to be via e-mail therefore limiting you to any anonymous confidentiality. There is the possibility for some of the drawings to be featured in a small book to be exhibited at the University of Greenwich’s Graduate Show 2020; if you’re ok with this, when submitting your drawings please state your name For any other questions dont hesitate to contact me at margazida@icloud.com
